# Problem 181: Employees Earning More Than Their Managers
![Easy](https://img.shields.io/badge/Level-Easy-brightgreen?style=for-the-badge) [![Problem Link](https://img.shields.io/badge/LeetCode-Link-orange?style=for-the-badge&logo=leetcode)](https://leetcode.com/problems/employees-earning-more-than-their-managers/description/)

```sql
SELECT
    -- Select the name of the employee (E)
    E.name AS Employee
FROM
    Employee E -- E represents the employee
INNER JOIN
    Employee M ON E.managerId = M.id -- M represents the manager
WHERE
    -- Filter for cases where the employee's salary is strictly greater than the manager's salary
    E.salary > M.salary;
```
