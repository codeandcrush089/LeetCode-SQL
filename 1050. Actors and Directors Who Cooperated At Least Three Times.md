# Problem 1050: Actors and Directors Who Cooperated At Least Three Times.md
![Easy](https://img.shields.io/badge/Level-Easy-brightgreen)

```sql
SELECT
    actor_id,
    director_id
FROM
    ActorDirector
-- 1. Group the rows by the unique pair of actor and director IDs
GROUP BY
    actor_id,
    director_id
-- 2. Filter the groups where the count of occurrences (cooperations) is 3 or more
HAVING
    COUNT(*) >= 3;
```
