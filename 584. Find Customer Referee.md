# Problem 584: Find Customer Referee
![Easy](https://img.shields.io/badge/Level-Easy-brightgreen?style=for-the-badge) [![Problem Link](https://img.shields.io/badge/LeetCode-Link-orange?style=for-the-badge&logo=leetcode)](https://leetcode.com/problems/find-customer-referee/description/)

```sql
SELECT
    name
FROM
    Customer
WHERE
    referee_id IS NULL  -- Condition 1: The customer was not referred by anyone (referee_id is NULL)
    OR referee_id != 2; -- Condition 2: The customer was referred by someone other than customer with ID 2
```
---
## üßê Query Explanation

### **1. `FROM Customer`**

Reads customer records from the `Customer` table.


### **2. `referee_id IS NULL`**

Selects customers who **were not referred by anyone**.


### **3. `OR referee_id != 2`**

Also selects customers who **were referred by someone other than customer ID 2**.


### **4. `SELECT name`**

Returns the **names** of customers that meet either condition.
