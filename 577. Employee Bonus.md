# Problem 577: Employee Bonus
![Easy](https://img.shields.io/badge/Level-Easy-brightgreen?style=for-the-badge) [![Problem Link](https://img.shields.io/badge/LeetCode-Link-orange?style=for-the-badge&logo=leetcode)](https://leetcode.com/problems/employee-bonus/description/)

```sql
SELECT
    E.name,
    B.bonus
FROM
    Employee E
LEFT JOIN
    Bonus B ON E.empId = B.empId
WHERE
    -- Condition 1: The employee has no bonus record (B.bonus IS NULL, covering 'John' and 'Brad').
    B.bonus IS NULL
    -- OR Condition 2: The employee has a bonus record, but the amount is less than 1000 (covering 'Dan').
    OR B.bonus < 1000;
```

### **1. `FROM Employee E`**

Starts with the **Employee** table so every employee is considered.


### **2. `LEFT JOIN Bonus B ON E.empId = B.empId`**

* Joins bonus information to employees.
* Keeps **all employees**, even those **without a bonus record**.
* Employees with no bonus get `NULL` in `B.bonus`.


### **3. `WHERE B.bonus IS NULL`**

Selects employees who **do not have any bonus**.


### **4. `OR B.bonus < 1000`**

Also selects employees whose **bonus exists but is less than 1000**.
